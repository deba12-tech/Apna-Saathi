Our Story

Apna Saathi was born from a simple observation: street food vendors across India were struggling to find reliable suppliers for quality ingredients at fair prices.

Founded in 2024, our platform combines cutting-edge technology with deep understanding of the Indian street food ecosystem to create a solution that benefits both vendors and suppliers.

Today, we serve thousands of vendors and suppliers across 15+ cities, helping them build stronger businesses and deliver better food to customers.
