Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a vendor?

Simply click on "Sign Up" and select "Street Food Vendor" as your role. Fill in your business details and you'll be ready to connect with suppliers.

How does the OCR bill upload work?

Upload a photo of your ingredient bill and our AI will automatically extract the items listed. This helps us match you with the right suppliers.

Is there a fee to use Apna Saathi?

Basic registration and platform usage is free. We charge a small commission on successful transactions between vendors and suppliers.

How do I verify suppliers?

All suppliers on our platform are verified through our quality assurance process. You can also check ratings and reviews from other vendors.
